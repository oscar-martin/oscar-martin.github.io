Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in Bingo for iOS.

The Data we collect automatically

We do collect, share and use user data as a result of the use of AdMob. User data usually contains IP address and mobile device identifiers (such as your device ID, advertising ID, MAC address, IMEI) but are not limited to that.

We do not use or save your personal data in Bingo for iOS per se. Bingo iOS does not contact with any server apart from the ones related to the ads service.

Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act

We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data about or direct or target interest based advertising to anyone under the age of 13.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to get in touch with us at oscarmartinvicente (at) gmail (dot) com
